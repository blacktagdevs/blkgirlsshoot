Three Days. Your People.

BLK GIRLS SHOOT is the annual gathering for Black women photographers who are serious about their craft and their business. Every year, we bring together a curated group of 80–100 women for live workshops, panels, and real conversations — no fluff, no filler, just the room you needed years ago.

BGS 2026 is happening September 4–6 in Atlanta. This is your weekend.

The Schedule

What's Waiting For You

Three days. Three different rooms. All of them built for you.

01

Friday, September 4

In Her Element

The Gallery. The Party. The Kickoff.

BGS kicks off Friday night with an opening that sets the tone for the entire weekend. VIP attendees submit their original photography work — selected pieces are professionally printed, mounted, and displayed throughout the venue as a full gallery exhibit.

Guests arrive to moody uplighting, a live DJ and violinist, passed appetizers, and a room full of Black women photographers ready to connect. The evening closes with a live winner announcement and a prize package that includes studio time, a free Adobe subscription, and equipment rental credit.

7:30–9PM

VIP Attendees · Gallery + Photography Competition + Opening Night Party

02

Saturday, September 5

Workshop Day

Camera Up. Shoot for Real.

Four live shooting stations. Four industry photographers. One day to actually create.

This is the hands-on heart of BGS. GA attendees choose 2 of 4 sessions. VIP gets all 4 with priority access. Show up ready to work, learn in real time, and leave with new skills and new images.

10AM–2PM

All Attendees · GA: Choose 2 Sessions · VIP: All 4 Sessions + Priority Access

03

Sunday, September 6

Business Summit

Build the Business Behind the Lens.

A full conference day centered around what it really takes to build a sustainable creative career.

From marketing and AI to pricing, systems, branding, and client experience — Sunday is about helping photographers stop guessing and start building smarter.

All Day

All Attendees · Panels + Keynotes + Industry Conversations
