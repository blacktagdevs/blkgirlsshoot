The Campaign

2026 · The Weekend · The Experience

The room we needed, so we built it.

Blk Girls Shoot started because too many of us were showing up to shoots, sets, conferences, and creative spaces as the only one in the room.

So we created our own.

BGS brings Black women photographers together for a weekend that's equal parts education, community, creativity, and business. This isn't about surface-level networking or sitting quietly in the back of a panel. It's about getting in the room, learning in real time, building relationships, and leaving better than you came.

Every year the community gets bigger. Every year the work gets better. And every year the room feels exactly like what we deserved all along.

What to Expect

Three days built with intention.

01

Friday · VIP Welcome Dinner

An intimate kickoff dinner for VIP attendees featuring guest speakers, conversation, and connection with women working across photography, creative direction, branding, and media.

This is where the introductions happen before the weekend even starts.

02

Saturday · Workshop Day

Hands-on workshops led by industry professionals actually working in the field right now.

No sitting through hours of talking at you. These are active sessions designed to help you grow your eye, your skill set, your confidence, and your workflow in real time.

GA attendees select two sessions. VIP attendees receive priority selection and access to all four.

03

Sunday · Business Conference

A full conference day centered around what it really takes to build a sustainable creative career.

From marketing and AI to pricing, systems, branding, and client experience – Sunday is about helping photographers stop guessing and start building smarter.

FAQ

Asked & answered.

Who is Blk Girls Shoot for? +

Black women photographers at every stage – whether you're still learning your camera or already shooting professionally full-time.

What's the difference between GA and VIP? +

GA includes Saturday workshop access and Sunday's business conference. VIP includes the Friday dinner, all four workshops with priority access, Sunday conference admission, and the sponsor swag bag.

What do the workshops look like? +

Hands-on learning experiences led by working creatives and photographers. These are active sessions where you'll actually be creating, learning, and applying techniques in real time.

Where is the event? +

Atlanta, Georgia. Confirmed attendees receive the official venue details closer to the event date.

Is lodging included? +

Travel and lodging are not included, but attendees will receive discounted hotel block information after registration.

Can I sponsor or partner? +

Yes. Submit an inquiry through the contact form and we'll send over partnership information.
