FAQ

Before you apply.

Is this paid? +

No, this internship is unpaid. It's structured to count for course credit or volunteer hours, and we provide documentation for both.

Who is this for? +

Senior-year college students or recent post-grads who are serious about marketing, ops, or partnerships and want a real case study on their resume.

How many hours per week? +

8–10 hours weekly across 12 weeks. One of those hours is a live mentor call (45–60 min); the rest is project work on your own schedule, plus a quick mid-week async check-in.

Is this remote? +

Yes, fully remote. Weekly calls are over Zoom. The only optional in-person component is attending a BGS shoot if you're local.

Do I need a marketing background? +

No formal background required. We care more about ownership, taste, and follow-through than what's already on your resume.

What will I actually own? +

A content calendar, a recurring "member of the week" spotlight series, an email reactivation campaign, a partnership pitch deck, and a capstone growth campaign you design and run. Every one of those is named and credited to you.

How are interns selected? +

Rolling applications. We review what you submit, then schedule a 20-minute call with shortlisted applicants before offering a spot.

When does the cohort start? +

June 1, 2026. Twelve weeks of work, wrapping mid-to-late August. We try to flex around your school calendar where we can.
